Privacy & Consent Management

It's crucial for website-owners to be aware of what cookies are being set by their sites, to be in control of what data is being sent and where, and to keep up to date with current policies and regulations. I have done plenty of quality-assurances and implementations of Cookie Management Platforms for sites. As well as reviews and recommendations on tracked data that are being collected and stored in various analytics tools. For B2B, e-commerce's and Swedish authorities.
The CMP's that I'm most familiar with is CookieBot and Cookie Information.

When Google's Consent Mode V2 was launched in the beginning of 2024, I worked with cookie management adjustments to function accordingly with Google's new requirements. I have written an article about how to easily verify your Consent Mode V2 implementation here
